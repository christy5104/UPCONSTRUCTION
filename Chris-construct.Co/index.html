Site overview and maintenance.

Maintenance plan is essential for ensuring that a construction or remodeling project runs smoothly and remains in good condition over time.

  • Evaluate the geographical and environmental conditions, including soil type.
  • Review local zoning laws, building codes, and any restrictions that may affect the project.
  • Create a detailed site plan that outlines the location of structures, access points, parking, landscaping, and utility connections.
  • Plan for the allocation of labor, materials, and equipment to ensure efficient progress.
